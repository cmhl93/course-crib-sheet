##	Lists

    * <ul>
  *	Ordered list		
    * <ol>
  *	Description list		
    * <dl>

##	Bold and italic
  *	Bold = `<strong>Example text</strong>`
  *	Italic = `<em>Example text</em>`
    * <b> similar to <strong>
      *	Not recommended
      *	Implies presentational preference
      *	Does not imply importance
  
##	Special characteristics
  *	Right single quote
    *	&rsquo;
  *	Left single quote
    *	&lsquo;
  *	Right double quote
    *	&rdquo;
  *	Left double quote
    *	&ldquo;
  *	Ampersand
    *	&amp;
  *	Greater than
    *	&gt;
  *	Less than
    *	&lt;
  *	Copyright
    *	&copy;
  *	Registered symbol
    *	&reg;
  *	Trademark symbol
    *	&trade;
